3. Scenario Handling
====================

A. Customer Cheque Received

Entry:
Cheque in Hand Dr
To Customer Cr

Status: in_hand

---

B. Cheque Bounce / Return

* Mark status = returned

Entry:
Customer Dr
To Cheque in Hand Cr

---

C. Supplier Cheque Issued

Entry:
Supplier Dr
To Cheque Payable Cr

Status: pending

---

D. Cheque Clearance

* Mark as cleared

Entry:
Cheque Payable Dr
To Bank Cr

---

E. Cheque Deposit (Customer Cheque to Bank)

Entry:
Bank Dr
To Cheque in Hand Cr

---

F. Cash Deposit

Entry:
Bank Dr
To Cash Cr

---

G. Bank Transfer

Entry:
Bank A Dr
To Bank B Cr
